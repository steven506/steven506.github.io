## Recommended Troubleshooting Approach

A strong troubleshooting process should follow this logic:

```text
1. Confirm the issue.
2. Confirm the impact.
3. Identify what changed.
4. Check system health.
5. Review logs.
6. Test connectivity.
7. Isolate the failing layer.
8. Apply the safest fix.
9. Validate recovery.
10. Document the root cause.
```
